CST 300 Course Discription

This course helps students identify and articulate personal, professional, and social goals. It also provides an integrated overview of the computer science and communication design majors and their requirements.
Students develop a plan for their learning goals. Students also learn writing, presentation, research and critical-thinking skills within the diversified fields of information technology and communication design.
Students will learn how to analyze, predict, and articulate trends in the academic, public service.
